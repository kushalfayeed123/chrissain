The $100K Challenge

Go From $1,000 to $100,000 in One Year

Are you ready to transform your financial future? The Chris Sain $100K Challenge is your roadmap to turning $1,000 into $100,000 in just one year. With proven strategies, disciplined investing, and clear guidelines, this challenge is designed to help you build wealth step by step.

Join the $100K Challenge Today

Chris Sain specializes in technical analysis and risk management, empowering you with the tools to confidently invest and build wealth in the stock market. His $100K Challenge serves as your ultimate blueprint for financial success, guiding and motivating you every step of the way.

Through his coaching, youll master the essentials of disciplined investing: starting small, thinking big, and making smart decisions to grow your portfolio. From building a diversified portfolio of proven stocks to exploring new opportunities, Chris provides the expertise and encouragement to keep you focused and confident.

But its not just about growing your wealthChris helps you prioritize financial health by tackling debt, building savings, and maximizing retirement contributions. With his guidance, youll develop the habits and discipline needed to achieve your goals.

With Chris, youll learn how to:

  • Read charts and analyze candles
  • Invest wisely and trade options
  • Manage risk effectively
  • Use stop-loss strategies
  • And much more

  • 1-on-1 and group coaching sessions

How The $100K Challenge Works

  • If eligible max out your Roth IRA contributions: $7,000 annually.
  • Build an emergency fund covering 3-6 months of expenses.
  • Pay off debt, including credit cards and car loans, to improve financial stability.
  • Invest Long Term consider ETFs.
  • Buy 125 shares minimum of each stock. Set a goal to accumulate 1,000 shares per stock.
  • Use a stop loss on all high-risk plays to limit potential losses.

Take control of your finances and achieve your goals. The $100K Challenge isnt just about making moneyits about learning discipline, building confidence, and securing your future.
